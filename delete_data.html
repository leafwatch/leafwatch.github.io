Delete Your Data – LeafWatch

At LeafWatch, we respect your privacy and give you control over the data you share with us. If you wish to have your data deleted, we make it easy for you to request this. Please follow the instructions below to request deletion of any data you have shared with us.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of any data you've shared with LeafWatch, simply follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at info@leafwatch.ai.
  2. In your email, please include:
    • The email address or username associated with your LeafWatch account.
    • A clear request for the deletion of your data, such as "I would like to delete all photos I have uploaded to LeafWatch."
  3. Our team will review your request and respond within [Insert Response Time, e.g., "30 days"].

What Data Is Deleted

We only store images that you have explicitly chosen to upload within the LeafWatch app. Once we receive your deletion request, all uploaded images associated with your account will be permanently removed from our servers.

What Data Is Retained

LeafWatch does not store any additional personal information or data beyond the images you choose to upload. Once your deletion request is processed, no other data will be retained.

Data Retention Period

After we receive your request, we will delete your images as soon as possible. In some cases, we may retain these images for a short period (up to [Insert Retention Period, e.g., "30 days"]) to ensure compliance with any legal requirements or pending support issues. Once this period has passed, your images will be permanently deleted from our systems.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team at info@leafwatch.ai.
