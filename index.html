Description

LeafWatch is a revolutionary mobile app that empowers farmers to monitor and protect their crops using cutting-edge AI technology. Simply take a photo of your crops, and our app will instantly analyze it for signs of pests, diseases, and watering issues.

Key features:

  • Instant pest and disease detection
  • Watering analysis (under/over watering identification)
  • Personalized recommendations for crop health
  • Historical data tracking and insights
