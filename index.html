We are launching LeafWatch.

We detect pests and diseases in your vine crops so you react on time. Powdery mildew, downy mildew, esca, phylloxera, etc. With a mobile app, artificial inteligence and a team of experts.

Act on time

Early detection -specially on fungal diseases- allows to react on time. Make a difference in both yield and quality, and reduce -or avoid altogether- the usage of pesticides.

Detect fast

Take pictures of your vine crops and have them analyzed by the system. Get results right away.

Effective

We have trained a computer vision algorithm on a huge image dataset to achieve great levels of precision. And we continuously improve it.

Easy to use

No need for special hardware, just your mobile phone equipped with a camera. Download the app and start scanning your crops.
